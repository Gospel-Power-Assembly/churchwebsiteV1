About Us

The Lord started dealing with His servant, Dr. Friday Bekee about the ministry while he was still lecturing in Port Harcourt. After series of dealings from the Lord and a protracted season of prayers, in June 1993, the Lord said to him, “Go to Ikeja and pioneer the work” This was a bit curious because as at then he did not know anywhere or anybody in Ikeja.

As he pondered about the possibility of this instruction, with his job and family in Port-Harcourt, he decided to step out in obedience and faith.

Welcome to Gospel Power Ministry

LEADING PEOPLE TO CHRIST AND PERFECTING PEOPLE IN CHRIST

Gospel Power Ministry is a place where God is awesomely worshiped and His presence manifests to bring about powerful transformation in the lives of people. A place where Gospel of Jesus Christ is preached through the power of the Holy Spirit and sinners find forgiveness through the blood of Jesus Christ. A place where the defeated, the dejected and demoralized find love, care, victory and fulfillment in life. A place where the depressed, distressed, diseased, and demoralized are healed and liberated by the power of God. A place to raise a people radiant with glory and contagious joy such that they will glow for Jesus wherever they go. A place where believers are taught trained and mobilized into a mighty army for Jesus Christ. A place where a mighty prayer army will be raised to pray for 24-hour a day, 7 days a week for people’s needs, the church and the world revival. A place where thousands of believers will be sent out for ministry projects and missionary activities all round the world. A place where people will come from all over the world to receive renewal and revival.

The Vision

The vision of the ministry is a progressive unfolding of the mind of the Lord. Its main thrust is: LEADING PEOPLE TO CHRIST AND PERFECTING PEOPLE IN CHRIST. We are persuaded to prioritize prayer and the Word as major pillars in accomplishing the vision.. Our Vision is our dream and its channels our mission.

Our Structure

The organizational structure is tailored after the vision of the ministry. The vision is leading people to Christ; perfecting people in Christ. Perfecting people in Christ involves maturing, training and equipping people in Christ. Leading people to Christ is the outcome of perfecting people in Christ. Perfecting people in Christ is a broad based assignment, a process which involves several spiritual disciplines that help us to develop our relationship with God and one another View Structure now.
